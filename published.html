A Chapbook

Many are the strives of man
Some strives with the dignity of their virtues while some lose it at the crossroad of decisions.Thus, only few gets to the destination of clarity.Blood, Sweat, Tears is a chapbook filled with therapeutic and moralistic poems. The poems are divided into three parts.
While some in the first part highlight the struggles, inhibitions, and hurdles of man, the second part explores the blissful concept of virtues.
And the third part reveals the hidden truth between the right and wrong. In summary, Blood, Tears is a chapbook you can feed your mind with anywhere, anytime, and in one sitting . Want to get a copy? Here you have it: https://selar.co/bloodsweetears

Tangled Dreams

While growing up, did you nurse multiple dreams? I do not mean the dreams you have when sleeping. I mean visions aspirations, and the 'what-I-want-to-be(s)'.
Well, I had a couple of them. I wanted to become all of them at once, and that was what got me tangled.
Tangled Dreams is a coming-of-age story that narrates the ordeals of a young girl with multiple talents, and tries tries to build on them all at once. But, is that really possible?
To know more, read yourself through: https://bit.ly/3Smz1AG
